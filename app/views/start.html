Bereavement Support Payment

Bereavement Support Payment provides financial support to people for a period after the death of a husband, wife or civil partner.

You can claim it if:

  • you are under State Pension age
  • you ordinarily live in the United Kingdom (UK), a country with which we have an agreement to pay certain UK benefits (a reciprocal agreement), or another EU or EEA country
  • the person who died paid enough National Insurance contributions

Applying takes around 30 minutes.

Before you start

You need:

  • the date your husband, wife or civil partner died
  • your husband's, wife's or civil partner's National Insurance number
  • your bank details
  • your marriage or civil partnership certificate
